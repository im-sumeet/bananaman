Dragon Ball Super: Zamas Arc

Zamas is a kai who killed his master, Gowas, because he wanted power and he wanted all mortals to be killed.

Goku Black is also the same kai who killed his master, Gowas, except Goku Black is from the future and stole Goku's body with the super dragon balls and he teamed up with Zamas because they both wanted the same thing, to kill all gods, dieties, and mortals in the universe.

Vegito is a fusion between Kakarot and Vegeta.

Vegito was supposed to be the one to kill Zamas and Goku Black but there is a timer for the fusion: 1 hour.
